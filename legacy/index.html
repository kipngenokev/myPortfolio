Approach

How I build

Every site I ship has to earn its keep — visually, technically, and commercially. Here's what that looks like in practice.

  1. 01

    User-focused

    Design starts with the people you're trying to reach. I plan content and flow before I touch a single pixel — so every section has a job.

  2. 02

    Performance-driven

    Fast sites convert. I write lean HTML, ship minimal JS, and aim for Lighthouse scores in the high 90s — by default, not as an afterthought.

  3. 03

    Built to scale

    Clean structure, sensible naming, and a design system you can actually edit. The site I hand over is the same one that's still working two years from now.
